Charlesquin Kemajou

AIMS & Heriot-Watt

ckemajou@aimsammi.org

Currently, I am a PhD researcher at the Heriot-Watt, Edinburgh, working with Marcelo Pereyra and Jean-Francois Giovannelli. My research lies at the intersection of Bayesian inference, image processing and machine learning. I am particularly interested in the theory of imaging inverse problems and applications in deconvolution problems in imaging.

Before joining Heriot-Watt university, I did three Masters dregrees. The first in Applied Mathematics at the Heriot-WattUniversity of Dschang, Cameroon. The second in Industrial Mathematics at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS), Cameroon. Finally, the last one in Machine learning at the African Masters in Machine Intelligence AMMI, Rwanda.
