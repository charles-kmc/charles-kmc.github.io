Currently, I am PhD student at the [Heriot-Watt university](https://www.hw.ac.uk/), working on Bayesian imaging inverse problems. I resumed the position since January 2021, and supervised by [Marcelo Pereyra]() at the University of Heriot-Watt and [Jean-Francois Geovannelli]() at the Université de Bordeaux, France.

Prior to this, I hold a Masters degree in Mathematical Sciences (Machine Intelligence) from African Masters of Machine intelligence (AMMI), Rwanda.
